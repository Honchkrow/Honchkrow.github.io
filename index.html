Wei Zhang

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the School of Control Science and Engineering, Shandong University. My cooperation mentor is Prof. Zhiping Liu. I received my Bachelor's degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2016. In 2022, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering under the guidance of Prof. Yanda Li and Prof. Xiaowo Wang from Tsinghua University.

Email  /  Google Scholar  /  Github

Research Interests

My research is in the area of computational biology and bioinformatics. I am engrossed in revealing the regulatory mechanism behind diseases with machine learning methods. I am also interested in developing algorithms and software to detect early cancer signals. I have developed several toolkits like esATAC, ARIC as well as cfDNApipe.
