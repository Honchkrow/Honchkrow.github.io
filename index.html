Wei Zhang

I am currently a research fellow at the School of Control Science and Engineering, Shandong University. I received my Bachelor's degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2016. In 2022, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering under the guidance of Prof. Yanda Li and Prof. Xiaowo Wang from Tsinghua University. My postdoctoral cooperation mentor is Prof. Zhiping Liu.

Research Interests

My current research interests focus on the application of artificial intelligence in biomedical engineering. I am dedicated to leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning methods to uncover the mechanisms underlying disease onset and progression, as well as to develop algorithms and software for the early diagnosis of complex diseases. Specifically, my research areas include:

(1) Understanding DNA language through LLMs;

(2) Integrating spatial omics data using graph neural networks and contrastive learning;

(3) Deciphering cancer mechanisms and enabling early detection with AI-driven approaches.
